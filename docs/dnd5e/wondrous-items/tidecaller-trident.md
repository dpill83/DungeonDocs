# Tidecaller Trident

You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic trident. You also have advantage on attack rolls made with this weapon while underwater.
This trident has 3 charges. You can expend 1 charge to cast
Control Water
(save DC 15) from the trident or 3 charges to cast
Tsunami
(save DC 15) from it instead. The trident regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn.
