You gain the following benefits.
Ability Score Increase.
Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.
Improved Intuition.
When you use the Hunter’s Intuition benefit of your Mark of Finding feat, you can roll 1d6 instead of 1d4.
Improved Finding.
When you use the Finder’s Magic benefit of your Mark of Finding to cast
Hunter’s Mark
without a spell slot, the range of the spell is doubled, and you can modify the spell so that the target can’t benefit from the Invisible condition for the duration.
