You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase**  
Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 30.

**Augmented Health**  
When you gain Temporary Hit Points, increase the number of Temporary Hit Points you gain by 5.

**Superior Recuperation**  
When you spend one or more Hit Dice to regain Hit Points, you can instead use the highest number possible for each die.
