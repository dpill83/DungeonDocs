Source: Eberron: Forge of the Artificer

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase**  
Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Improved Intuition**  
When you use the Cunning Intuition benefit of your Mark of Shadow feat, you can roll 1d6 instead of 1d4.

**Improved Shadow**  
When you use the Shape Shadows benefit of your Mark of Shadow feat to cast Invisibility without a spell slot, you cast the spell as its level 3 version.
