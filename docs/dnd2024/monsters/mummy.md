    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-actions">Actions</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><strong><em>Multiattack.</em></strong> The mummy makes two Rotting Fist attacks and uses Dreadful Glare.</p>
<p><strong><em>Rotting Fist.</em></strong> <em>Melee Attack Roll:</em> +5, reach 5 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 8 (1d10 + 3) Bludgeoning damage plus 10 (3d6) Necrotic damage. If the target is a creature, it is cursed. While cursed, the target can't regain Hit Points, its Hit Point maximum doesn't return to normal when finishing a Long Rest, and its Hit Point maximum decreases by 10 (3d6) every 24 hours that elapse. A creature dies and turns to dust if reduced to 0 Hit Points by this attack.</p>
<p><strong><em>Dreadful Glare.</em></strong> <em>Wisdom Saving Throw:</em> DC 11, one creature the mummy can see within 60 feet. <em>Failure:</em> The target has the Frightened condition until the end of the mummy's next turn. <em>Success:</em> The target is immune to this mummy's Dreadful Glare for 24 hours.</p>
