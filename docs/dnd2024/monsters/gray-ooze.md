<p><strong><em>Amorphous.</em></strong> The ooze can move through a space as narrow as 1 inch without expending extra movement to do so.</p>
<p><strong><em>Corrosive Form.</em></strong> Nonmagical ammunition is destroyed immediately after hitting the ooze and dealing any damage. Any nonmagical weapon takes a cumulative −1 penalty to attack rolls immediately after dealing damage to the ooze and coming into contact with it. The weapon is destroyed if the penalty reaches −5.
