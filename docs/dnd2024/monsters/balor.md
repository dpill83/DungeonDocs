    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-traits">Traits</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><strong><em>Death Throes.</em></strong> The balor explodes when it dies. <em>Dexterity Saving Throw:</em> DC 20, each creature in a 30-foot Emanation originating from the balor. <em>Failure:</em> 31 (9d6) Fire damage plus 31 (9d6) Force damage. <em>Success:</em> Half damage. <em>Failure or Success:</em> If the balor dies outside the Abyss, it gains a new body instantly, reviving with all its Hit Points somewhere in the Abyss.</p>
<p><strong><em>Fire Aura.</em></strong> At the end of each of the balor's turns, each creature in a 5-foot Emanation originating from the balor takes 13 (3d8) Fire damage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Legendary Resistance (3/Day).</em></strong> If the balor fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.</p>
<p><strong><em>Magic Resistance.</em></strong> The balor has Advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.</p>

<p><strong><em>Multiattack.</em></strong> The balor makes one Flame Whip attack and one Lightning Blade attack.</p>
<p><strong><em>Flame Whip.</em></strong> <em>Melee Attack Roll:</em> +14, reach 30 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 18 (3d6 + 8) Force damage plus 17 (5d6) Fire damage. If the target is a Huge or smaller creature, the balor pulls the target up to 25 feet straight toward itself, and the target has the Prone condition.</p>
<p><strong><em>Lightning Blade.</em></strong> <em>Melee Attack Roll:</em> +14, reach 10 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 21 (3d8 + 8) Force damage plus 22 (4d10) Lightning damage, and the target can't take Reactions until the start of the balor's next turn.</p>
    </div>
