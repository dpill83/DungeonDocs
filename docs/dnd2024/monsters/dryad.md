<p><strong><em>Thorn Burst.</em></strong> <em>Ranged Attack Roll:</em> +6, range 60 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 7 (1d6 + 4) Piercing damage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Spellcasting.</em></strong> The dryad casts one of the following spells, requiring no Material components and using Charisma as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 14): <br><br>
&emsp;<strong>At Will:</strong> <em>Animal Friendship, Charm Monster</em> (lasts 24 hours; ends early if the dryad casts the spell again), <em>Druidcraft</em> <br><br>
&emsp;<strong>1/Day Each:</strong> <em>Entangle, Pass without Trace</em></p>
