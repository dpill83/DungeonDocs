    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-actions">Actions</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><strong><em>Multiattack.</em></strong> The dragon makes three Rend attacks. It can replace one attack with a use of (A) Repulsion Breath or (B) Spellcasting to cast <em>Guiding Bolt</em> (level 2 version).</p>
<p><strong><em>Rend.</em></strong> <em>Melee Attack Roll:</em> +16, reach 15 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 18 (2d8 + 9) Slashing damage plus 9 (2d8) Lightning damage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Lightning Breath (Recharge 5–6).</em></strong> <em>Dexterity Saving Throw:</em> DC 23, each creature in a 120-foot-long, 10-foot-wide Line. <em>Failure:</em> 82 (15d10) Lightning damage. <em>Success:</em> Half damage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Repulsion Breath.</em></strong> <em>Strength Saving Throw:</em> DC 23, each creature in a 30-foot Cone. <em>Failure:</em> The target is pushed up to 60 feet straight away from the dragon and has the Prone condition.</p>
<p><strong><em>Spellcasting.</em></strong> The dragon casts one of the following spells, requiring no Material components and using Charisma as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 22, +14 to hit with spell attacks): <br><br>
&emsp;<strong>At Will:</strong> <em>Detect Magic</em>, <em>Guiding Bolt</em> (level 2 version), <em>Shapechange</em> (Beast or Humanoid form only, no Temporary Hit Points gained from the spell, and no Concentration or Temporary Hit Points required to maintain the spell), <em>Speak with Animals</em>, <em>Thaumaturgy</em> <br><br>
&emsp;<strong>1/Day Each:</strong> <em>Detect Thoughts</em>, <em>Control Water</em>, <em>Scrying</em>, <em>Water Breathing</em></p>
    </div>
  </section>
  <section class="monster-section monster-section-legendary-actions" aria-labelledby="section-legendary-actions">
    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-legendary-actions">Legendary Actions</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><em>Legendary Action Uses: 3 (4 in Lair). Immediately after another creature's turn, the dragon can expend a use to take one of the following actions. The dragon regains all expended uses at the start of each of its turns.</em></p>
<p><strong><em>Guiding Light.</em></strong> The dragon uses Spellcasting to cast <em>Guiding Bolt</em> (level 2 version).</p>
<p><strong><em>Pounce.</em></strong> The dragon moves up to half its Speed, and it makes one Rend attack.</p>
<p><strong><em>Thunderclap.</em></strong> <em>Constitution Saving Throw:</em> DC 22, each creature in a 20-foot-radius Sphere centered on a point the dragon can see within 120 feet. <em>Failure:</em> 13 (3d8) Thunder damage, and the target has the Deafened condition until the end of its next turn.</p>
