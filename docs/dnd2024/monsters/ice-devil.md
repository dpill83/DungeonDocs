    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-actions">Actions</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><strong><em>Multiattack.</em></strong> The devil makes three Ice Spear attacks. It can replace one attack with a Tail attack.</p>
<p><strong><em>Ice Spear.</em></strong> <em>Melee or Ranged Attack Roll:</em> +10, reach 5 ft. or range 30/120 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 14 (2d8 + 5) Piercing damage plus 10 (3d6) Cold damage. Until the end of its next turn, the target can't take a Bonus Action or Reaction, its Speed decreases by 10 feet, and it can move or take one action on its turn, not both. <em>Hit or Miss:</em> The spear magically returns to the devil's hand immediately after a ranged attack.</p>
<p><strong><em>Tail.</em></strong> <em>Melee Attack Roll:</em> +10, reach 10 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 15 (3d6 + 5) Bludgeoning damage plus 18 (4d8) Cold damage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Ice Wall (Recharge 6).</em></strong> The devil casts <em>Wall of Ice</em> (level 8 version), requiring no spell components and using Intelligence as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 17).</p>
