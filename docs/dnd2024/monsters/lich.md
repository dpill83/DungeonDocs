    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-traits">Traits</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><strong><em>Legendary Resistance (4/Day, or 5/Day in Lair).</em></strong> If the lich fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.</p>
<p><strong><em>Spirit Jar.</em></strong> If destroyed, the lich reforms in 1d10 days if it has a spirit jar, reviving with all its Hit Points. The new body appears in an unoccupied space within the lich's lair.</p>

  <section class="monster-section monster-section-actions" aria-labelledby="section-actions">
    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-actions">Actions</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><strong><em>Multiattack.</em></strong> The lich makes three attacks, using Eldritch Burst or Paralyzing Touch in any combination.</p>
<p><strong><em>Eldritch Burst.</em></strong> <em>Melee or Ranged Attack Roll:</em> +12, reach 5 ft. or range 120 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 31 (4d12 + 5) Force damage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Paralyzing Touch.</em></strong> <em>Melee Attack Roll:</em> +12, reach 5 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 15 (3d6 + 5) Cold damage, and the target has the Paralyzed condition until the start of the lich's next turn.</p>
<p><strong><em>Spellcasting.</em></strong> The lich casts one of the following spells, using Intelligence as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 20): <br><br>
&emsp;<strong>At Will:</strong> <em>Detect Magic, Detect Thoughts, Dispel Magic, Fireball</em> (level 5 version), <em>Invisibility, Lightning Bolt</em> (level 5 version), <em>Mage Hand, Prestidigitation</em> <br><br>
&emsp;<strong>2/Day Each:</strong> <em>Animate Dead, Dimension Door, Plane Shift</em> <br><br>
&emsp;<strong>1/Day Each:</strong> <em>Chain Lightning, Finger of Death, Power Word Kill, Scrying</em></p>
    </div>
  </section>
  <section class="monster-section monster-section-reactions" aria-labelledby="section-reactions">
    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-reactions">Reactions</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><strong><em>Protective Magic.</em></strong> The lich casts <em>Counterspell</em> or <em>Shield</em> in response to the spell's trigger, using the same spellcasting ability as Spellcasting.</p>
    </div>
  </section>
  <section class="monster-section monster-section-legendary-actions" aria-labelledby="section-legendary-actions">
    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-legendary-actions">Legendary Actions</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><em>Legendary Action Uses: 3 (4 in Lair). Immediately after another creature's turn, the lich can expend a use to take one of the following actions. The lich regains all expended uses at the start of each of its turns.</em></p>
<p><strong><em>Deathly Teleport.</em></strong> The lich teleports up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space it can see, and each creature within 10 feet of the space it left takes 11 (2d10) Necrotic damage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Disrupt Life.</em></strong> <em>Constitution Saving Throw:</em> DC 20, each creature that isn't an Undead in a 20-foot Emanation originating from the lich. <em>Failure:</em> 31 (9d6) Necrotic damage. <em>Success:</em> Half damage. <em>Failure or Success:</em> The lich can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.</p>
<p><strong><em>Frightening Gaze.</em></strong> The lich casts <em>Fear</em>, using the same spellcasting ability as Spellcasting. The lich can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.</p>
    </div>
