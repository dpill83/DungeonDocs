    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-traits">Traits</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><strong><em>Demonic Restoration.</em></strong> If the marilith dies outside the Abyss, its body dissolves into ichor, and it gains a new body instantly, reviving with all its Hit Points somewhere in the Abyss.</p>
<p><strong><em>Magic Resistance.</em></strong> The marilith has Advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.</p>
<p><strong><em>Reactive.</em></strong> The marilith can take one Reaction on every turn of combat.</p>

  <section class="monster-section monster-section-actions" aria-labelledby="section-actions">
    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-actions">Actions</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><strong><em>Multiattack.</em></strong> The marilith makes six Pact Blade attacks and uses Constrict.</p>
<p><strong><em>Pact Blade.</em></strong> <em>Melee Attack Roll:</em> +10, reach 5 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 10 (1d10 + 5) Slashing damage plus 7 (2d6) Necrotic damage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Constrict.</em></strong> <em>Strength Saving Throw:</em> DC 17, one Medium or smaller creature the marilith can see within 5 feet. <em>Failure:</em> 15 (2d10 + 4) Bludgeoning damage. The target has the Grappled condition (escape DC 14), and it has the Restrained condition until the grapple ends.</p>
    </div>
  </section>
