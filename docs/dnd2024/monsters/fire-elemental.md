    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-traits">Traits</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><strong><em>Fire Aura.</em></strong> At the end of each of the elemental's turns, each creature in a 10-foot Emanation originating from the elemental takes 5 (1d10) Fire damage. Creatures and flammable objects in the Emanation start burning.</p>
<p><strong><em>Fire Form.</em></strong> The elemental can move through a space as narrow as 1 inch without expending extra movement to do so, and it can enter a creature's space and stop there. The first time it enters a creature's space on a turn, that creature takes 5 (1d10) Fire damage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Illumination.</em></strong> The elemental sheds Bright Light in a 30-foot radius and Dim Light for an additional 30 feet.</p>
<p><strong><em>Water Susceptibility.</em></strong> The elemental takes 3 (1d6) Cold damage for every 5 feet the elemental moves in water or for every gallon of water splashed on it.</p>
