<p><strong><em>Multiattack.</em></strong> The vrock makes two Shred attacks.</p>
<p><strong><em>Shred.</em></strong> <em>Melee Attack Roll:</em> +6, reach 5 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 10 (2d6 + 3) Piercing damage plus 10 (3d6) Poison damage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Spores (Recharge 6).</em></strong> <em>Constitution Saving Throw:</em> DC 15, each creature in a 20-foot Emanation originating from the vrock. <em>Failure:</em> The target has the Poisoned condition and repeats the save at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. While Poisoned, the target takes 5 (1d10) Poison damage at the start of each of its turns. Emptying a flask of Holy Water on the target ends the effect early.</p>
<p><strong><em>Stunning Screech (1/Day).</em></strong> <em>Constitution Saving Throw:</em> DC 15, each creature in a 20-foot Emanation originating from the vrock (demons succeed automatically). <em>Failure:</em> 10 (3d6) Thunder damage, and the target has the Stunned condition until the end of the vrock's next turn.</p>
