<p><strong><em>Tentacle.</em></strong> <em>Melee Attack Roll:</em> +7, reach 60 ft. <em>Hit:</em> The target has the Grappled condition (escape DC 14) from one of six tentacles, and the target has the Poisoned condition until the grapple ends. <br><br>
&emsp;The tentacle can be damaged, freeing a creature it has Grappled when destroyed (AC 20, HP 10, Immunity to Poison and Psychic damage). Damaging the tentacle deals no damage to the roper, and a destroyed tentacle regrows at the start of the roper's next turn.</p>
<p><strong><em>Reel.</em></strong> The roper pulls each creature Grappled by it up to 30 feet straight toward it.</p>
