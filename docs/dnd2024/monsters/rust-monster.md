    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-actions">Actions</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><strong><em>Multiattack.</em></strong> The rust monster makes one Bite attack and uses Antennae twice.</p>
<p><strong><em>Bite.</em></strong> <em>Melee Attack Roll:</em> +3, reach 5 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 5 (1d8 + 1) Piercing damage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Antennae.</em></strong> The rust monster targets one nonmagical metal object—armor or a weapon—worn or carried by a creature within 5 feet of itself. <em>Dexterity Saving Throw:</em> DC 11, the creature with the object. <em>Failure:</em> The object takes a −1 penalty to the AC it offers (armor) or to its attack rolls (weapon). Armor is destroyed if the penalty reduces its AC to 10, and a weapon is destroyed if its penalty reaches −5. The penalty can be removed by casting the <em>Mending</em> spell on the armor or weapon.</p>
<p><strong><em>Destroy Metal.</em></strong> The rust monster touches a nonmagical metal object within 5 feet of itself that isn't being worn or carried. The touch destroys a 1-foot Cube of the object.</p>
    </div>
