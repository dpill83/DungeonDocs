    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-traits">Traits</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><strong><em>Barbed Hide.</em></strong> At the start of each of its turns, the devil deals 5 (1d10) Piercing damage to any creature it is grappling or any creature grappling it.</p>
<p><strong><em>Diabolical Restoration.</em></strong> If the devil dies outside the Nine Hells, its body disappears in sulfurous smoke, and it gains a new body instantly, reviving with all its Hit Points somewhere in the Nine Hells.</p>
<p><strong><em>Magic Resistance.</em></strong> The devil has Advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.</p>

<p><strong><em>Claws.</em></strong> <em>Melee Attack Roll:</em> +6, reach 5 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 10 (2d6 + 3) Piercing damage. If the target is a Large or smaller creature, it has the Grappled condition (escape DC 13) from both claws.</p>
<p><strong><em>Tail.</em></strong> <em>Melee Attack Roll:</em> +6, reach 10 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 14 (2d10 + 3) Slashing damage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Hurl Flame.</em></strong> <em>Ranged Attack Roll:</em> +5, range 150 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 17 (5d6) Fire damage. If the target is a flammable object that isn't being worn or carried, it starts burning.</p>
