  <section class="monster-section monster-section-actions" aria-labelledby="section-actions">
    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-actions">Actions</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><strong><em>Multiattack.</em></strong> The dragon makes three Rend attacks. It can replace one attack with a use of (A) Sleep Breath or (B) Spellcasting to cast <em>Scorching Ray</em> (level 3 version).</p>
<p><strong><em>Rend.</em></strong> <em>Melee Attack Roll:</em> +14, reach 15 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 19 (2d10 + 8) Slashing damage plus 7 (2d6) Fire damage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Fire Breath (Recharge 5–6).</em></strong> <em>Dexterity Saving Throw:</em> DC 21, each creature in a 90-foot-long, 5-foot-wide Line. <em>Failure:</em> 58 (13d8) Fire damage. <em>Success:</em> Half damage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Sleep Breath.</em></strong> <em>Constitution Saving Throw:</em> DC 21, each creature in a 90-foot Cone. <em>Failure:</em> The target has the Incapacitated condition until the end of its next turn, at which point it repeats the save. <em>Second Failure:</em> The target has the Unconscious condition for 10 minutes. This effect ends for the target if it takes damage or a creature within 5 feet of it takes an action to wake it.</p>
<p><strong><em>Spellcasting.</em></strong> The dragon casts one of the following spells, requiring no Material components and using Charisma as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 20): <br><br>
&emsp;<strong>At Will:</strong> <em>Detect Magic</em>, <em>Minor Illusion</em>, <em>Scorching Ray</em> (level 3 version), <em>Shapechange</em> (Beast or Humanoid form only, no Temporary Hit Points gained from the spell, and no Concentration or Temporary Hit Points required to maintain the spell), <em>Speak with Animals</em> <br><br>
&emsp;<strong>1/Day Each:</strong> <em>Control Weather</em>, <em>Detect Thoughts</em></p>
    </div>
  </section>
  <section class="monster-section monster-section-legendary-actions" aria-labelledby="section-legendary-actions">
    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-legendary-actions">Legendary Actions</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><em>Legendary Action Uses: 3 (4 in Lair). Immediately after another creature's turn, the dragon can expend a use to take one of the following actions. The dragon regains all expended uses at the start of each of its turns.</em></p>
<p><strong><em>Blazing Light.</em></strong> The dragon uses Spellcasting to cast <em>Scorching Ray</em> (level 3 version).</p>
<p><strong><em>Pounce.</em></strong> The dragon moves up to half its Speed, and it makes one Rend attack.</p>
<p><strong><em>Scorching Sands.</em></strong> <em>Dexterity Saving Throw:</em> DC 20, one creature the dragon can see within 120 feet. <em>Failure:</em> 36 (8d8) Fire damage, and the target's Speed is halved until the end of its next turn. <em>Failure or Success:</em> The dragon can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.</p>
    </div>
