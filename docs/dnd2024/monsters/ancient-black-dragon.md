    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-actions">Actions</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><strong><em>Multiattack.</em></strong> The dragon makes three Rend attacks. It can replace one attack with a use of Spellcasting to cast <em>Acid Arrow</em> (level 4 version).</p>
<p><strong><em>Rend.</em></strong> <em>Melee Attack Roll:</em> +15, reach 15 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 17 (2d8 + 8) Slashing damage plus 9 (2d8) Acid damage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Acid Breath (Recharge 5–6).</em></strong> <em>Dexterity Saving Throw:</em> DC 22, each creature in a 90-foot-long, 10-foot-wide Line. <em>Failure:</em> 67 (15d8) Acid damage. <em>Success:</em> Half damage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Spellcasting.</em></strong> The dragon casts one of the following spells, requiring no Material components and using Charisma as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 21, +13 to hit with spell attacks): <br><br>
&emsp;<strong>At Will:</strong> <em>Acid Arrow</em> (level 4 version), <em>Detect Magic</em>, <em>Fear</em> <br><br>
&emsp;<strong>1/Day Each:</strong> <em>Create Undead</em>, <em>Speak with Dead</em>, <em>Vitriolic Sphere</em> (level 5 version)</p>
    </div>
  </section>
  <section class="monster-section monster-section-legendary-actions" aria-labelledby="section-legendary-actions">
    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-legendary-actions">Legendary Actions</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><em>Legendary Action Uses: 3 (4 in Lair). Immediately after another creature's turn, the dragon can expend a use to take one of the following actions. The dragon regains all expended uses at the start of each of its turns.</em></p>
<p><strong><em>Cloud of Insects.</em></strong> <em>Dexterity Saving Throw:</em> DC 21, one creature the dragon can see within 120 feet. <em>Failure:</em> 33 (6d10) Poison damage, and the target has Disadvantage on saving throws to maintain Concentration until the end of its next turn. <em>Failure or Success:</em> The dragon can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.</p>
<p><strong><em>Frightful Presence.</em></strong> The dragon uses Spellcasting to cast <em>Fear</em>. The dragon can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.</p>
<p><strong><em>Pounce.</em></strong> The dragon moves up to half its Speed, and it makes one Rend attack.</p>
