<p><strong><em>Radiant Horn.</em></strong> <em>Melee Attack Roll:</em> +7, reach 5 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 9 (1d10 + 4) Radiant damage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Spellcasting.</em></strong> The unicorn casts one of the following spells, requiring no spell components and using Charisma as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 14): <br><br>
&emsp;<strong>At Will:</strong> <em>Detect Evil and Good, Druidcraft</em> <br><br>

<p><strong><em>Unicorn's Blessing (3/Day).</em></strong> The unicorn touches another creature with its horn and casts <em>Cure Wounds</em> or <em>Lesser Restoration</em> on that creature, using the same spellcasting ability as Spellcasting.</p>

  <section class="monster-section monster-section-legendary-actions" aria-labelledby="section-legendary-actions">
    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-legendary-actions">Legendary Actions</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><em>Legendary Action Uses: 3. Immediately after another creature's turn, the unicorn can expend a use to take one of the following actions. The unicorn regains all expended uses at the start of each of its turns.</em></p>
<p><strong><em>Charging Horn.</em></strong> The unicorn moves up to half its Speed without provoking Opportunity Attacks, and it makes one Radiant Horn attack.</p>
<p><strong><em>Shimmering Shield.</em></strong> The unicorn targets itself or one creature it can see within 60 feet of itself. The target gains 10 (3d6) Temporary Hit Points, and its AC increases by 2 until the end of the unicorn's next turn. <br><br>
&emsp;The unicorn can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.</p>
