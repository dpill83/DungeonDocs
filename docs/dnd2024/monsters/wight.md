<p><strong><em>Multiattack.</em></strong> The wight makes two attacks, using Necrotic Sword or Necrotic Bow in any combination. It can replace one attack with a use of Life Drain.</p>
<p><strong><em>Necrotic Sword.</em></strong> <em>Melee Attack Roll:</em> +4, reach 5 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 6 (1d8 + 2) Slashing damage plus 4 (1d8) Necrotic damage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Necrotic Bow.</em></strong> <em>Ranged Attack Roll:</em> +4, range 150/600 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 6 (1d8 + 2) Piercing damage plus 4 (1d8) Necrotic damage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Life Drain.</em></strong> <em>Constitution Saving Throw:</em> DC 13, one creature within 5 feet. <em>Failure:</em> 6 (1d8 + 2) Necrotic damage, and the target's Hit Point maximum decreases by an amount equal to the damage taken. <br><br>
&emsp;A Humanoid slain by this attack rises 24 hours later as a <strong>Zombie</strong> under the wight's control, unless the Humanoid is restored to life or its body is destroyed. The wight can have no more than twelve zombies under its control at a time.</p>
