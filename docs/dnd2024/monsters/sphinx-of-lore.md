    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-traits">Traits</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><strong><em>Inscrutable.</em></strong> No magic can observe the sphinx remotely or detect its thoughts without its permission. Wisdom (Insight) checks made to ascertain its intentions or sincerity are made with Disadvantage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Legendary Resistance (3/Day, or 4/Day in Lair).</em></strong> If the sphinx fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.</p>

  <section class="monster-section monster-section-actions" aria-labelledby="section-actions">
    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-actions">Actions</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><strong><em>Multiattack.</em></strong> The sphinx makes three Claw attacks.</p>
<p><strong><em>Claw.</em></strong> <em>Melee Attack Roll:</em> +8, reach 5 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 14 (3d6 + 4) Slashing damage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Mind-Rending Roar (Recharge 5–6).</em></strong> <em>Wisdom Saving Throw:</em> DC 16, each enemy in a 300-foot Emanation originating from the sphinx. <em>Failure:</em> 35 (10d6) Psychic damage, and the target has the Incapacitated condition until the start of the sphinx's next turn.</p>
<p><strong><em>Spellcasting.</em></strong> The sphinx casts one of the following spells, requiring no Material components and using Intelligence as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 16): <br><br>
&emsp;<strong>At Will:</strong> <em>Detect Magic</em>, <em>Identify</em>, <em>Mage Hand</em>, <em>Minor Illusion</em>, <em>Prestidigitation</em> <br><br>
&emsp;<strong>1/Day Each:</strong> <em>Dispel Magic</em>, <em>Legend Lore</em>, <em>Locate Object</em>, <em>Plane Shift</em>, <em>Remove Curse</em>, <em>Tongues</em></p>
    </div>
  </section>
  <section class="monster-section monster-section-legendary-actions" aria-labelledby="section-legendary-actions">
    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-legendary-actions">Legendary Actions</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><em>Legendary Action Uses: 3 (4 in Lair). Immediately after another creature's turn, the sphinx can expend a use to take one of the following actions. The sphinx regains all expended uses at the start of each of its turns.</em></p>
<p><strong><em>Arcane Prowl.</em></strong> The sphinx can teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space it can see, and it makes one Claw attack.</p>
<p><strong><em>Weight of Years.</em></strong> <em>Constitution Saving Throw:</em> DC 16, one creature the sphinx can see within 120 feet. <em>Failure:</em> The target gains 1 Exhaustion level. While the target has any Exhaustion levels, it appears 3d10 years older. <em>Failure or Success:</em> The sphinx can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.</p>
