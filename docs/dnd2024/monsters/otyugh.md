    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-actions">Actions</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><strong><em>Multiattack.</em></strong> The otyugh makes one Bite attack and two Tentacle attacks.</p>
<p><strong><em>Bite.</em></strong> <em>Melee Attack Roll:</em> +6, reach 5 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 12 (2d8 + 3) Piercing damage, and the target has the Poisoned condition. Whenever the Poisoned target finishes a Long Rest, it is subjected to the following effect. <em>Constitution Saving Throw:</em> DC 15. <em>Failure:</em> The target's Hit Point maximum decreases by 5 (1d10) and doesn't return to normal until the Poisoned condition ends on the target. <em>Success:</em> The Poisoned condition ends.</p>
<p><strong><em>Tentacle.</em></strong> <em>Melee Attack Roll:</em> +6, reach 10 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 12 (2d8 + 3) Piercing damage. If the target is a Medium or smaller creature, it has the Grappled condition (escape DC 13) from one of two tentacles.</p>
<p><strong><em>Tentacle Slam.</em></strong> <em>Constitution Saving Throw:</em> DC 14, each creature Grappled by the otyugh. <em>Failure:</em> 16 (3d8 + 3) Bludgeoning damage, and the target has the Stunned condition until the start of the otyugh's next turn. <em>Success:</em> Half damage only.</p>
