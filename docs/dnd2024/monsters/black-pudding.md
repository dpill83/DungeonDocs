  <section class="monster-section monster-section-traits" aria-labelledby="section-traits">
    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-traits">Traits</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><strong><em>Amorphous.</em></strong> The pudding can move through a space as narrow as 1 inch without expending extra movement to do so.</p>
<p><strong><em>Corrosive Form.</em></strong> A creature that hits the pudding with a melee attack roll takes 4 (1d8) Acid damage. Nonmagical ammunition is destroyed immediately after hitting the pudding and dealing any damage. Any nonmagical weapon takes a cumulative −1 penalty to attack rolls immediately after dealing damage to the pudding and coming into contact with it. The weapon is destroyed if the penalty reaches −5. The penalty can be removed by casting the <em>Mending</em> spell on the weapon. <br><br>
&emsp;In 1 minute, the pudding can eat through 2 feet of nonmagical wood or metal.</p>
<p><strong><em>Spider Climb.</em></strong> The pudding can climb difficult surfaces, including along ceilings, without needing to make an ability check.</p>

Nonmagical armor worn by the target takes a −1 penalty to the AC it offers. The armor is destroyed if the penalty reduces its AC to 10. <p><strong><em>Split.</em></strong> <em>Trigger:</em> While the pudding is Large or Medium and has 10+ Hit Points, it becomes Bloodied or is subjected to Lightning or Slashing damage. <em>Response:</em> The pudding splits into two new <strong>Black Puddings</strong>. Each new pudding is one size smaller than the original pudding and acts on its Initiative. The original pudding's Hit Points are divided evenly between the new puddings (round down).</p>
