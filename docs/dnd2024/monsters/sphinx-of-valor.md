    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-traits">Traits</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><strong><em>Inscrutable.</em></strong> No magic can observe the sphinx remotely or detect its thoughts without its permission. Wisdom (Insight) checks made to ascertain its intentions or sincerity are made with Disadvantage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Legendary Resistance (3/Day, or 4/Day in Lair).</em></strong> If the sphinx fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.</p>

  <section class="monster-section monster-section-actions" aria-labelledby="section-actions">
    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-actions">Actions</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><strong><em>Multiattack.</em></strong> The sphinx makes two Claw attacks and uses Roar.</p>
<p><strong><em>Claw.</em></strong> <em>Melee Attack Roll:</em> +12, reach 5 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 20 (4d6 + 6) Slashing damage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Roar (3/Day).</em></strong> The sphinx emits a magical roar. Whenever it roars, the roar has a different effect, as detailed below (the sequence resets when it takes a Long Rest):</p>
<p><strong><em>First Roar.</em></strong> <em>Wisdom Saving Throw:</em> DC 20, each enemy in a 500-foot Emanation originating from the sphinx. <em>Failure:</em> The target has the Frightened condition for 1 minute.</p>
<p><strong><em>Second Roar.</em></strong> <em>Wisdom Saving Throw:</em> DC 20, each enemy in a 500-foot Emanation originating from the sphinx. <em>Failure:</em> The target has the Paralyzed condition, and it repeats the save at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. After 1 minute, it succeeds automatically.</p>
<p><strong><em>Third Roar.</em></strong> <em>Constitution Saving Throw:</em> DC 20, each enemy in a 500-foot Emanation originating from the sphinx. <em>Failure:</em> 44 (8d10) Thunder damage, and the target has the Prone condition. <em>Success:</em> Half damage only.</p>
<p><strong><em>Spellcasting.</em></strong> The sphinx casts one of the following spells, requiring no Material components and using Wisdom as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 20): <br><br>
&emsp;<strong>At Will:</strong> <em>Detect Evil and Good</em>, <em>Thaumaturgy</em> <br><br>
&emsp;<strong>1/Day Each:</strong> <em>Detect Magic</em>, <em>Dispel Magic</em>, <em>Greater Restoration</em>, <em>Heroes' Feast</em>, <em>Zone of Truth</em></p>
    </div>
  </section>
  <section class="monster-section monster-section-legendary-actions" aria-labelledby="section-legendary-actions">
    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-legendary-actions">Legendary Actions</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><em>Legendary Action Uses: 3 (4 in Lair). Immediately after another creature's turn, the sphinx can expend a use to take one of the following actions. The sphinx regains all expended uses at the start of each of its turns.</em></p>
<p><strong><em>Arcane Prowl.</em></strong> The sphinx can teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space it can see, and it makes one Claw attack.</p>
<p><strong><em>Weight of Years.</em></strong> <em>Constitution Saving Throw:</em> DC 16, one creature the sphinx can see within 120 feet. <em>Failure:</em> The target gains 1 Exhaustion level. While the target has any Exhaustion levels, it appears 3d10 years older. <em>Failure or Success:</em> The sphinx can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.</p>
    </div>
