<p><strong><em>Constrict.</em></strong> <em>Strength Saving Throw:</em> DC 15, one Large or smaller creature the salamander can see within 10 feet. <em>Failure:</em> 11 (2d6 + 4) Bludgeoning damage plus 7 (2d6) Fire damage.
