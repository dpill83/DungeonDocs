    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><strong><em>Multiattack.</em></strong> The dragon makes three Rend attacks. It can replace one attack with a use of Spellcasting to cast <em>Shatter</em>.</p>
<p><strong><em>Rend.</em></strong> <em>Melee Attack Roll:</em> +12, reach 10 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 16 (2d8 + 7) Slashing damage plus 5 (1d10) Lightning damage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Lightning Breath (Recharge 5–6).</em></strong> <em>Dexterity Saving Throw:</em> DC 19, each creature in a 90-foot-long, 5-foot-wide Line. <em>Failure:</em> 60 (11d10) Lightning damage. <em>Success:</em> Half damage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Spellcasting.</em></strong> The dragon casts one of the following spells, requiring no Material components and using Charisma as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 18): <br><br>
&emsp;<strong>At Will:</strong> <em>Detect Magic</em>, <em>Invisibility</em>, <em>Mage Hand</em>, <em>Shatter</em> <br><br>
&emsp;<strong>1/Day Each:</strong> <em>Scrying</em>, <em>Sending</em></p>

  <section class="monster-section monster-section-legendary-actions" aria-labelledby="section-legendary-actions">
    <h2 class="monster-section-title" id="section-legendary-actions">Legendary Actions</h2>
    <div class="monster-section-content">
<p><em>Legendary Action Uses: 3 (4 in Lair). Immediately after another creature's turn, the dragon can expend a use to take one of the following actions. The dragon regains all expended uses at the start of each of its turns.</em></p>
<p><strong><em>Cloaked Flight.</em></strong> The dragon uses Spellcasting to cast <em>Invisibility</em> on itself, and it can fly up to half its Fly Speed. The dragon can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.</p>
<p><strong><em>Sonic Boom.</em></strong> The dragon uses Spellcasting to cast <em>Shatter</em>. The dragon can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.</p>
<p><strong><em>Tail Swipe.</em></strong> The dragon makes one Rend attack.</p>
