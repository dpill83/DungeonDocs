<p><strong><em>Bite.</em></strong> <em>Melee Attack Roll:</em> +4, reach 5 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 5 (1d6 + 2) Piercing damage plus 2 (1d4) Poison damage, and the target has the Poisoned condition until the start of the ettercap's next turn.</p>
<p><strong><em>Claw.</em></strong> <em>Melee Attack Roll:</em> +4, reach 5 ft. <em>Hit:</em> 7 (2d4 + 2) Slashing damage.</p>
<p><strong><em>Web Strand (Recharge 5–6).</em></strong> <em>Dexterity Saving Throw:</em> DC 12, one Large or smaller creature the ettercap can see within 30 feet. <em>Failure:</em> The target has the Restrained condition until the web is destroyed (AC 10; HP 5; Vulnerability to Fire damage; Immunity to Bludgeoning, Poison, and Psychic damage).</p>
