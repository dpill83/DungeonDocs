# Rope Of Mending

This 50-foot coil of rope can repair itself when cut into any number of smaller pieces. As a Magic action, you can cause all pieces of the rope that are in contact with each other and not otherwise in use to knit back together. A
Rope of Mending
is forever shortened if a section of it is lost or destroyed.
