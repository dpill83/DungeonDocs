# Feat Greater Mark Of Scribing

You gain the following benefits.
Ability Score Increase.
Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.
Improved Intuition.
When you use the Gifted Scribe benefit of your Mark of Scribing feat, you can roll 1d6 instead of 1d4.
Inspired Scribing.
When you cast
Comprehend Languages
, you can modify the spell to cause a sigil to appear in the air above a creature you can see within 30 feet of yourself, which lasts for the duration of the spell. While the sigil persists, your enemies within 30 feet of the creature must spend 2 feet of movement for every 1 foot they move closer to that creature. The sigil disappears if the creature makes an attack roll, casts a spell, or deals damage.
