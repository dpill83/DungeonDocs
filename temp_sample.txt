<p data-content-chunk-id="7b2ac9db-775d-4d8e-b5cb-1a94e9878a54">This thick book is bound in dark leather, crisscrossed with inlaid silver lines suggesting a map or chart. When found, the book contains the following spells, which are wizard spells for you while you are attuned to the book: <a class="tooltip-hover spell-tooltip" href="https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2358-arcane-gate" data-tooltip-href="//www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2358-tooltip?disable-webm=1&amp;disable-webm=1">arcane gate</a>, <a class="tooltip-hover spell-tooltip" href="https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2068-dimension-door" data-tooltip-href="//www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2068-tooltip?disable-webm=1&amp;disable-webm=1">dimension door</a>, <a class="tooltip-hover spell-tooltip" href="https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2119-gate" data-tooltip-href="//www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2119-tooltip?disable-webm=1&amp;disable-webm=1">gate</a>, <a class="tooltip-hover spell-tooltip" href="https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2195-misty-step" data-tooltip-href="//www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2195-tooltip?disable-webm=1&amp;disable-webm=1">misty step</a>, <a class="tooltip-hover spell-tooltip" href="https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2206-plane-shift" data-tooltip-href="//www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2206-tooltip?disable-webm=1&amp;disable-webm=1">plane shift</a>, <a class="tooltip-hover spell-tooltip" href="https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2276-teleportation-circle" data-tooltip-href="//www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2276-tooltip?disable-webm=1&amp;disable-webm=1">teleportation circle</a>, and <a class="tooltip-hover spell-tooltip" href="https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2304-word-of-recall" data-tooltip-href="//www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2304-tooltip?disable-webm=1&amp;disable-webm=1">word of recall</a>. It functions as a spellbook for you.</p>
<p data-content-chunk-id="6977452f-1a05-444d-b60d-77a1e17fa947">While you are holding the book, you can use it as a spellcasting focus for your wizard spells.</p>
<p data-content-chunk-id="3fca1198-d64f-4fce-b5a6-38149b15310e">The book has 3 charges, and it regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. You can use the charges in the following ways while holding it:</p>
<ul data-content-chunk-id="cc0f711d-1cc1-407a-8096-db7943cd2a4a">
<li data-content-chunk-id="84a1d7c5-e1bd-4178-a893-c9f70b92b9fd">If you spend 1 minute studying the book, you can expend 1 charge to replace one of your prepared wizard spells with a different spell in the book. The new spell must be of the conjuration school.</li>
<li data-content-chunk-id="6110b178-f2cd-47f1-936d-269a9a95ca35">When you are hit by an attack, you can use your reaction to expend 1 charge to teleport up to 10 feet to an unoccupied space you can see. If your new position is out of range of the attack, it misses you.</li>
</ul>
